Frozen lasagna recipe

The lasagna is a delicious Italian dish that everyone enjoys. Although this recipe can be hard to make, you can go to the store and buy one from the freezer, and now the only step is to put it in the oven!

Ingredients

  • Your favourite store-bought frozen lasagna
  • Taste buds :)

Steps

  1. Place it in a glass dish about its size
  2. Put it in the oven at 190 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes
  3. Take it out and put it on the plate
  4. Enjoy!
